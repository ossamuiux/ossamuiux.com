영상정보

We will be creating a tiling ground texture from start to finish and by the end of the tutorial, you will know how to create textures similar to the images above! The final texture seen in these screenshots is made right before your eyes without skipping a single second. You will learn the theory behind creating good textures, fast and efficient workflows for making organic shapes of all kinds , and how to quickly iterate on your work. By the end of this tutorial you will be an organic texturing pro!
